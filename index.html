My research lies at the intersection of harmonic analysis and functional analysis, with a particular focus on non-standard function spaces. I bring nearly a decade of university-level teaching experience, active research output, graduate supervision, and academic leadership experience.

About

Research Profile

Research in harmonic analysis, functional analysis, theory of function spaces, grand function spaces, weighted norm inequalities, and complex interpolation.

Academic Leadership

Served as Head of Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Riphah International University, with experience in curriculum development and academic management.

Teaching & Supervision

Nearly a decade of university teaching experience, including supervision of Ph.D. and M.Phil. students.

Research Interests

Harmonic Analysis

Riesz potentials, Marcinkiewicz integrals, Calderón-Zygmund operators, and boundedness problems.

Function Spaces

Variable exponent spaces, Herz spaces, Hardy spaces, Morrey spaces, grand spaces, and net spaces.

Operator Theory

Kernel operators, maximal operators, potential operators, interpolation theory, and weighted inequalities.
